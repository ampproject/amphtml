## Example

The `width` and `height` attributes determine the aspect ratio of the player embedded in responsive layouts.

Example:

```html
<amp-reach-player
  data-embed-id="default"
  layout="responsive"
  width="560"
  height="315"
>
</amp-reach-player>
```
