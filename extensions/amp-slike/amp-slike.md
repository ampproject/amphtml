## Example

The `width` and `height` attributes determine the aspect ratio of the player embedded in responsive layout.

Example:

```html
<amp-slike
  data-apikey="33502051"
  data-videoid="1281471"
  layout="responsive"
  width="480"
  height="270"
>
</amp-slike>
```

## Attributes

<table>
  <tr>
    <td width="40%"><strong>data-apikey</strong></td>

  <tr>
