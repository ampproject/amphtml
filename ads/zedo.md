## Configuration

For semantics of configuration, please contact support@zedo.com.


### Required parameters

- `data-super-id`
- `data-network`
- `data-placement-id`
- `data-channel`
- `data-publisher`
- `data-dim`

### Optional parameters

- `data-charset`
- `data-callback`
- `data-renderer`
