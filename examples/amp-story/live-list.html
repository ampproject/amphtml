The 2018 Daegu International Bodypainting Festival brought all the colors of the rainbow to Daegu, South Korea this weekend. Sixty-one teams representing 14 countries participated in the fantasy exhibition, where makeup artists turned models into walking works of art for the public’s viewing pleasure.
