Located next to London's Belgrave district Buckingham Palace offers a unique opportunity to stay in a piece of England's history. Traditionally the home of the UK's ruling sovereign since 1837 a small number of rooms are now available for rent. Spacious high-ceilinged rooms offer four poster beds and views across St James Park. WiFi is limited.
