The best Fortnite Creative codes

PCGamesN

Fortnite transformed from a PvE experience into a battle royale game. Now it’s also a gaming platform that lets players construct their dream mini-games and share them with others. Here are some of the best.

DUST II – 5253-4025-7629

This recreation of CS:GO’s most iconic map is perfect, right down to the last detail. Load this up with some friends and face-off in a game of team deathmatch.

SKY SNIPES – 1743-2048-4798

This clever multiplayer map is made of floating islands. Players must use Grapplers and Bouncers to fly around - with help from a low-gravity modifier - eliminating each other with Hunting Rifles.

MW2 TERMINAL REMAKE – 6412-7533-9638

Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2’s Terminal is easily one of the best maps in the game. The huge airliner is the only element of the original map that looks awkward in Fortnite form, but overall it’s a great remake.

TWISTED TEMPLE – 1217-3985-3745

This temple-themed deathmatch map boasts plenty of space for vertical play, which you can make the most of with Fortnite’s building mechanics.

BOUNCE PAD DEATHMATCH – 8792-1332-7866

How do you make deathmatch even better? Simple: add bounce pads. Lots of them. Load this Creative code up with some friends and see who’s better at dodging bullets.

WARM UP COURSE – 6561-6398-2653

Here’s an edit course that’s designed to get you flexing all of your Fortnite muscles before heading into a battle royale: shooting, editing, building, and parkour. Practice makes perfect!

CIZZORZDEATHRUN CHALLENGE – 0940-9970-7913

This challenging parkour map was dreamt up by FaZe Cizzorz and is every bit as challenging as you would expect from a pro player. Don’t expect to finish this in your first hundred attempts.

EPIC BRIDGE – 9795-3537-9919

There’s no game mode attached to this creation, but we think you’ll agree that just walking around this massive, ornate bridge is spectacular. While it’s not built for multiplayer, a battle for control of the bridge would be incredible.

THPS WAREHOUSE – 1654-0324-7788

The addition of the Hoverboard to Fortnite means we get Fortnite Creative maps inspired by Tony Hawk’s Pro Skater. This simple tribute to the Warehouse level lets you shred with ramps and halfpipes.

RIFTY’S RACE – 1330-3984-1519

If you’ve been practising your Mario Kart then you’ll breeze through this ATK race. Just be sure to bring some friends along so you can show off your powersliding prowess.
